The ''!-PDFDocument-!'' fixture allows for the text within a PDF file to be checked.
#
!2 Example
#
|!-fitlibrary.PDFDocument-!|
|''open''|Submission.pdf|
|''text''|''contains''|Thanks for your submission|
|''number of pages''|'''is'''|2|
|''select page''|1|
|''paragraph below heading''|Follow Up:|'''is'''|We will contact you in the next few days to provide feedback on your submission.|
|''select page''|2|
|''text''|''contains''|Fine print details|
|''paragraph below heading''|LEGAL|'''is'''|Please follow all legal requirements to the letter.|
|''paragraph below heading''|UPDATES|'''is'''|Changes to these requirements may be made by us at any time.|
|''show pdf as image''|
|''close PDF file''|
#
!2 Commands
#
 * Start the fixture:

|!-fitlibrary.PDFDocument-!|

 * Open a PDF file:

|''open''|Submission.pdf|

 * Check that the string appears somewhere in the text in the whole document:

|''text''|''contains''|Thanks for your submission|

 * Confirm the number of pages:

|''number of pages''|''is''|2|

 * Select a specific page:

|''select page''|1|

 * Select the text below a given heading and up to the next heading (can also use '''contains''' and '''matches''':

|''paragraph below heading''|Follow Up:|''is''|We will contact you in the next few days to provide feedback on your submission.|

|''select page''|2|

 * Check that the string appears somewhere in the text of the second page:

|''text''|''contains''|Fine print details|

 * Dump an image of the PDF and include it in the storytest report:

|''show pdf as image''|

 * Finish processing the pdf by closing the file:

|''close PDF file''|
#
!2 Implementation
#
This makes use of the apache open-source ''pdfbox'' system.

See http://incubator.apache.org/pdfbox/ for further details.
